How it works

A straightforward 5-step flow from reservation to final billing.

  1. Reserve online — choose your car, period & branch. (Front-end card validation)
  2. Pick up — bring your license and booking ID to the selected branch.
  3. Enjoy the ride — drive the EV with confidence; support is available.
  4. Return & inspect — staff check the vehicle at the designated car park.
  5. Final bill — rental plus any damages are finalized at the counter.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to reserve?

Yes. Create an account (stored locally for demo) and log in to proceed to Booking.

Is payment real?

No. This is a front-end demo with card number validation only (no backend processing).

What happens to my data?

Accounts are stored in your browser’s localStorage; sessions use sessionStorage. Clear your browser data to remove them.

Where can I pick up the car?

You can choose between the Central Branch or East Branch during booking.
